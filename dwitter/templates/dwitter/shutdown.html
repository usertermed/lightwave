Lightwave Shutdown Announcement

Important message from the founder

I started developing Lightwave back in June 2023, and the closed beta opened in August 2024. Since then, there have been 586 bugfixes and 10 major updates.

Although this project has been a great success, it has gotten too large for me to manage, and server costs have skyrocketed.

On November 12th at 6:00 PM CST, all user data will be deleted and the website will show this screen for the remainder of the week. All source code will be published on the Lightwave Media GitHub.

Thank you all for your continuous support,

Damien Sullins

Founder of Lightwave Media Group
